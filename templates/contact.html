Frequently Asked Questions

What are your shipping options and delivery times?
We offer express shipping worldwide with insurance. Domestic orders arrive within 2-3 business days, international orders within 5-7 business days. All jewelry comes with secure packaging and tracking.
Do you offer custom jewelry design services?
Yes! Our master jewelers create bespoke pieces tailored to your vision. We offer consultation sessions, 3D design previews, and use only the finest materials. Custom orders typically take 4-6 weeks to complete.
What is your return and exchange policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy for unworn items in original condition. All returns are processed within 5-7 business days. Custom and personalized items are final sale unless there's a manufacturing defect.
Are your jewelry pieces certified and authentic?
Absolutely! All our jewelry comes with certificates of authenticity. Diamonds are GIA certified, precious metals are hallmarked, and designer pieces include brand authentication. We guarantee 100% authenticity.
Do you offer jewelry repair and maintenance services?
Yes, we provide comprehensive jewelry services including cleaning, resizing, stone replacement, and restoration. Our certified jewelers ensure your pieces maintain their beauty and value for years to come.
How can I become a VIP member?
VIP membership is available for customers with annual purchases over $5,000. Benefits include exclusive access to new collections, personal shopping services, special events, and priority customer support.
